You are choleric person
Choleric is emotional, prone to impulsive actions, energetic and initiative. The predominance of excitement over inhibition, characteristic of choleric, is clearly manifested in incontinence, impetuosity, irascibility and irritability. Hence the expressive facial expressions, hasty speech, sharp gestures, unrestrained movements. Feelings and emotions of a choleric person are strong, they arise quickly and are clearly manifested, the mood changes dramatically. The imbalance inherent in a choleric person is clearly manifested in his activities: he takes up new things with passion, works with enthusiasm, easily overcoming difficulties. This makes him successful in solving shock life tasks, in the solution of which the sanguine person feels like a “fish in water”. Routine causes a sharp decline in him: the rise and inspiration disappear - the mood drops sharply.
In dealing with people, the choleric admits harshness, irritability and emotional incontinence. His excessive straightforwardness, irascibility, harshness and intolerance often leads to conflict situations. The conflicts arising around the choleric are aggravated by the fact that in actions he is an adventurer and he is constantly drawn to risks, often not justified.The high speed of mental processes allows a choleric person to quickly cope with complex tasks in conditions of lack of time, which significantly distinguishes him from representatives of other temperaments. The important differences between choleric people include forgiving and easy tolerance of criticism.
