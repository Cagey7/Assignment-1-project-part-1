You are melancholic person
A melancholic is a sensitive and refined person. The melancholic is characterized by restraint and muffledness of motor skills and speech, depth and stability of feelings with their weak external expression. Emotions in a melancholic are predominantly negative, and a tendency towards sadness dominates among them.In work, the melancholic is usually passive. Feelings and emotional states in him differ in depth and duration - melancholic people are easily vulnerable, they can hardly bear resentment and grief. The melancholic is prone to isolation and loneliness, in the company of new or unfamiliar people, they are often embarrassed and show great awkwardness. Everything new and unusual causes a state of inhibition in melancholic people. Communication for a melancholic is complicated by his touchiness and suspiciousness.
The melancholic does not tolerate stress well, his reaction to the lack of time is traumatic - he gets tired from the very thought of tough deadlines. The atmosphere of conflict for a melancholic is simply unbearable.In this familiar and calm environment, the melancholic feels calm and works very productively. He's not just a man of his word, he's super required.
