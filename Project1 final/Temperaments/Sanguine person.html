You are sanguine person
The sanguine person is cheerful, quickly navigates in a new environment, easily switches from one type of activity to another, but does not like monotonous work. A sanguine person speaks quickly and is accompanied by expressive facial expressions and gestures. In a new environment, a sanguine person manifests himself as an active and energetic person, while routine causes him indifference, boredom and lethargy. We can say that a sanguine person is an addictive nature, capable of quick switching. Even if something does not work out in accordance with his desires, the sanguine person quickly switches to something else, which allows him to maintain a positive emotional background in almost any situation. The sanguine person accepts everything that happens easily, he is unable to indulge in gloomy thoughts, anxiety or fear for a long time.
A sanguine person is a master of establishing contacts, he does not tolerate loneliness and deprivation of communication for him is a severe punishment. When putting forward ideas, a sanguine person is not too concerned about their reliability and feasibility. Rather, he likes the very process of using his imagination. The sanguine person has developed feelings of their importance, dignity and self-confidence, which allows him to maintain composure and self-control in difficult situations.
